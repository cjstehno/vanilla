        • long toMillis()

          Converts the time span value to milliseconds. At spans of more than DAYS, the conversion is approximate and based on a pure 7-day week, 30-day month, and 365-day year. Generally it is not advisable to convert a span of larger scope than DAYS. Also note that span values are converted to "long" values during the conversion so some precision may be lost.

          Returns:
          the time span converted to milliseconds.
